National Identification Number (NIN) – New Enrollment Guide

Follow the steps below to complete your NIN enrollment. Ensure all required documents are ready before your appointment.

STEP 1: Prepare Required Documents

Adults (18+) born in Nigeria:

  • Court Affidavit (Required)
  • VALID Digital NPC Certificate Number (Attestation)

Attestation Link ($20): https://attestation.nationalpopulation.gov.ng/

Assistance Available:

  • Court Affidavit Assistance: $25
  • Digital NPC Attestation Assistance: $10
  • Both Services Bundle: $50

Link for assistance information: https://forms.gle/tNj8B3kqQE3DcTB16

Adults/Children born outside Nigeria:

  • Foreign Birth Attestation (Required)
  • Attestation Link ($20): https://foreignbirth.nationalpopulation.gov.ng/
  • Assistance Available: $10

Children (0–17) born in Nigeria:

  • VALID Digital NPC Registration Certificate
  • Note: Online service currently in development; assistance unavailable.

STEP 2: Schedule Appointment & Complete Form

  • Booking Fee: $30 (Non-refundable unless cancelled before appointment)
  • Book Appointment: https://calendly.com/iqid_usa/minneapolis
  • Download Enrollment Form: KSQ Enrollment Form (PDF)

STEP 3: Attend Appointment

  • Complete biometrics capture at the office (Golden Valley)
  • Enrollment Fee:
    • Adults: $70 + tax
    • Minors: $50 + tax
  • Payment Method: Visa or Mastercard ONLY

Items to Bring

  • Nigerian Passport (current or expired)
  • For foreign-born children: Birth certificate + parents’ Nigerian passports
  • Visa or Mastercard for payment

Payment Information

  • Cash App: $AbisolaFJNIN
